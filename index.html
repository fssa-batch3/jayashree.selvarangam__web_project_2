why choose us?

Quality, care, and preparation of coffee beans! All of our coffee beans are locally supplied and freshly roasted on site by our supplier. Our baristas at The Caffine factory are all trained in the handling and preparation of our gourmet coffee beans. We grind and brew a variety of coffee blends daily, ensuring you a fresh cup of coffee regardless of what suits your taste buds.

Our famously known house blend, coffee, is a delectable medium-bodied coffee that is roasted locally. Try this caffeinated blend hot or iced, and feel the magic of The caffine factory!
